Find us

We train and play match (both men's and women's) at the same ground.

Our address is:
Carshalton Road, Woodmansterne, SM7 3HU

By Train

We recommend people travel to either Coulsdon Town or Coulsdon South train stations. A lot of our players drive and are happy to give lifts from the station to/from the pitches.

Coulsdon South/Town are only a couple of stops from East Croydon (or Purley). It couldn't be easier!

By Car

Putting the postcode into any SatNav / maps app seems to work pretty well. Our only recommendation is that if you are driving from the south, it is much easier to drive via Woodmansterne Lane / Little Woodcote Ln / Woodcote Grove Rd, than to go through Chipstead (which may be maps' default).

There is ample parking at the ground.
