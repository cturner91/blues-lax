Bluesfest!

The greatest weekend of your year!

Bluesfest is the annual drinking lacrosse tournament held at Blues' home venue in June.
30 teams competing in 10-minute games over 2 days, and when it's all said and done (and Spencer have won), you can retire to the comfort of your tent that hasn't been used or washed since last year's Bluesfest.

Get ready for some great weather* to enjoy some great lacrosse** and enjoy Blues players' great hosting*** at the home of SEMLA's greatest club****.

* Great weather not guaranteed.

** Great lacrosse not guaranteed.

*** Great hosting not guaranteed (and is frankly unlikely).

**** Greatest club title is beyond dispute.
