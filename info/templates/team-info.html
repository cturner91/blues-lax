Blues Team Info

All teams train on Tuesdays 7.30pm - 9.00pm at our home pitch.

SEMLA Premier Division

Our men's team compete in the SEMLA Premier Division, the highest level of lacrosse in the south of England. the men's team is a blend of experienced senior players and youthful recent graduates. The men's team made it to the semi-finals of the Senior Flags competition in the 2021-22 season, losing to eventual winners Spencer.

The aim is for a top 3 finish in the 2022-23 season.

Local League

The men's team is also involved in the London Local League, division 2. These games are generally better-natured and a bit less competitive. Whether you're looking to push for silverware, or just looking for a runaround (and a pint after), you will have plenty of opportunities at Blues.

Match Info

Unless otherwise communicated, all men's games will be played on Saturdays with a 2pm face-off. Always aim to be at the venue at least an hour before face-off.

Training Info

The men's team train every week on Tuesdays from 7.30pm-9.00pm.

We recognise that this time may not be suitable for everyone, but attendance to training is required for selection in matches. Exceptions can be made for pre-existing arrangements that cannot be changed (e.g. work), but these are the exceptions and not the norm. Players who wish to be selected regularly need to be training regularly.
