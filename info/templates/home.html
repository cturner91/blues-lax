Walcountian Blues Lacrosse Club

{% include "partials/home-carousel.html" %}

Walcountian Blues is the longest-running lacrosse club in south London with successful men's and women's teams - and a reputation for being the most sociable club in the south.

We welcome players of all ages and abilities. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to get in touch!

Find us

Carshalton Road, Woodmansterne, SM7 3HU

We are close to Coulsdon South and Coulsdon Town train stations, which are only a couple of stops from East Croydon and Purley stations. A lot of our players drive and are happy to give lifts from the station to/from the pitches.

Teams Info

Both men's and women's teams have training on Tuesday evenings at 7.30pm until 9pm. This usually runs every week between September until the last match of the season (~April).

Summer Training

There is curently some informal training being led over the summer on Tuesdays at 7pm (gotta love those summer evenings). However, this is not every week, so if you want to come down, please do get in touch first!
